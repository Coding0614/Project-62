About My Photo Gallery

My name is Arnav. This is my photo gallery. The photos are displayed in 2 ways: Grid View and Slide view. The grid view will show 5 of the 10 images on this website in a straight line. On the other hand, the slide view will show the rest of the images, and on the click of any arrow, you will see the next or previous image. I hope you like my photo gallery, and tell me how I can improve it. Have fun!
